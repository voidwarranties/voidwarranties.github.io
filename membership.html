VoidWarranties is accessible to everyone. You can visit the hackerspace before choosing to become a member. Due to physical constraints please note that we may enforce a member cap if necessary.

Procedure

To become a member find 2 existing members who support your membership. You can find these members by participating and being present in the hackerspace. You can then fill in the membership form and hand it over to an administrative member. Other members may object during a one-month waiting period. Assuming no one objects, you're now a member!

Membership fees

Fees are paid every month.

  • Adults pay €12.50
  • Students under 25 pay €10
  • Minors pay half their age in euro's (a 10 year old pays €5)
